Privacy Policy

How the East Coast Rowing Council collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: August 2021  |  Adapted from the Irish Coastal Rowing Federation Privacy Policy

Overview

The East Coast Rowing Council (ECRC) is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors to this website and members of our affiliated clubs. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to keep it safe.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information through contact forms and club registration processes. The types of data we collect include:

  • Name and postal address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • IP address and browsing activity on this website

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide relevant information about ECRC activities
  • Notify members and clubs of organisational updates, events, and regatta information
  • Maintain contact as specified in any agreements with affiliated clubs

Data Protection & Sharing

Your personal information will not be sold or rented to any third parties for marketing purposes. Where data is shared with third parties, it is only for the purposes of operating this website or administering ECRC activities, and all such parties are required to keep your information confidential.

We protect your data through secure servers and SSL certificates. However, you should be aware that transmission of information over the internet is never completely secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, modification of, or deletion of your personal information held by the ECRC. To exercise these rights, please contact us at eastcoastrowingireland@gmail.com.

You must opt in before receiving any marketing communications from the ECRC.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and for analytics. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the site.

External Links

This privacy policy applies only to the ECRC website. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites linked from our pages.

Minors

Individuals under the age of 16 require parental or guardian permission before submitting personal information through this website.

International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred outside the EU/Ireland for the purposes of website hosting and administration. In all such cases, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at eastcoastrowingireland@gmail.com.

East Coast Rowing Council

The governing body for coastal rowing on the East Coast of Ireland, covering counties Dublin, Wicklow, and North Wexford. Affiliated to the Irish Coastal Rowing Federation.
